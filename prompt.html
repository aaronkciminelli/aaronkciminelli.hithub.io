Art Prompt Generator

Use this "Mad Libs" style generator to inspire your next creation.

To get a more entertaining and unexpected prompt, simply click on the checkbox labeled "Hide template."

If you're having trouble thinking of an idea for any of the inputs, just click "generate input" and one will be provided for you.

Once you click on the submit button, you may need to deselect the Hide Template option to view your results. (I am working on a fix for this).
